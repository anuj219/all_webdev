Recently, a global IT outage caused by a faulty update from CrowdStrike led to widespread disruptions in Microsoft systems. This incident affected various sectors, including airlines, banks, and retail. Delta Air Lines, for instance, had to cancel over 5,000 flights and manually reboot numerous systems1. Microsoft deployed hundreds of IT experts to restore services and collaborated with other cloud providers like AWS and Google Cloud to mitigate the impact2.
